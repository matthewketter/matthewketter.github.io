Privacy

Last updated 10 May 2026

This page (matthewketter.github.io) is a static personal site. It does not set cookies, run analytics, or collect any personal information from visitors. There is no account system, no contact form, and no tracking pixels.

What this site does not do

  • No cookies or local storage of any kind.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or visitor tracking.
  • No advertising, retargeting, or third-party trackers.
  • No contact form, mailing list, or account signup.

Hosting

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information such as IP addresses and request headers for security, abuse prevention, and aggregate usage statistics. That collection is governed by the GitHub General Privacy Statement.

Fonts

The site loads the Inter typeface from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com / fonts.gstatic.com). When your browser fetches the font files, Google receives standard request information including your IP address. See the Google Privacy Policy and the Google Fonts privacy FAQ for details.

External links

This site links out to other sites I run or use, including quillmark.ai, complianceflag.com, GitHub, and LinkedIn. Those sites have their own privacy practices and are not covered by this notice.

Quillmark products

This privacy notice covers only this personal portfolio. Quillmark products and services (including the Quillmark website and Compliance Flag) are governed by their own privacy policies, available on each product's site.

Contact

Questions about this notice? Email github@ketter.dev.
